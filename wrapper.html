FluxWallet Wrapped ETH (FWETH) is an ERC20 token minted and destroyed at a 1:1 ratio, representing the ETH held within the FWETH Smart Contract.
Simply put, it gives ETH the powers of an ERC20 token - programmable, multi-functional - but still most importantly, immutable, decentralised and secure.

There's two parts to how FWETH works: Wrapping ETH and Unwrapping FWETH. Let's quickly go through both.

Wrapping ETH
  • 1. You deposit ETH into the FWETH Smart Contract, via this Dapp interface
  • 2. Once transactions are confirmed, you receive FWETH, minted 1:1 with the ETH you deposited
  • 3. You use the FWETH however applicable to your requirement
  • 4. You come back here to unwrap your FWETH once you are done

Unwrapping FWETH
  • 1. You unwrap FWETH tokens via this Dapp interface
  • 2. FWETH tokens are destroyed from the Current Supply, at a 1:1 ratio with the ETH you receive from unwrapping the FWETH tokens.
  • 3. You have ETH in your wallet, ready for regular use or trading!

FluxWallet Wrapped ETH is made available for use by the general public, via this Dapp interface.
Right now, FWETH is not listed on any exchanges or DEXs - however, You are welcome to wrap and unwrap ETH as you need / see fit.
